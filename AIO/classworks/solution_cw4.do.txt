/*4. First, consider a single product model. Estimate the following regression:
(a) Raw price elasticity
(b) Add to the previous regression, model (code_model) fixed-effects
(c) Add to the previous regression, year fixed-effects
(d) Add to the previous regression, market fixed-effects
Include the output in the Word file and comment on the results obtained.*/

* 4.a
ge log_price = log(price)
ge log_quantity = log(quantity)

regress log_quantity log_price

* 4.b
regress log_quantity log_price i.code_model
testparm i.code_model

reghdfe log_quantity log_price, absorb(code_model)

* 4.c
reghdfe log_quantity log_price i.year, absorb(code_model)

testparm i.year

reghdfe log_quantity log_price, absorb(year code_model)


* 4.d 
reghdfe log_quantity log_price i.market, absorb(code_model year)

testparm i.market

reghdfe log_quantity log_price, absorb(year code_model market)

*5. Add in the last regression a control for the population and include it in the Word file. Is the coefficient identified? Why?
reghdfe log_quantity log_price pop, absorb(year code_model market)

* 6. model in characteristics-space- 4 different regressions

ge sj = quantity/pop
bys market year: egen sj_tot = total(sj)
ge s0 = 1- sj_tot

ge y = log(sj) - log(s0)
* 6.a
regress y price
* 6.b
reghdfe y price, absorb(code_model)
* 6.c
reghdfe y price, absorb(code_model year)
* 6.d
reghdfe y price, absorb(code_model year market)

*7. Now include product characteristics in the last regression
reghdfe y price cy we pl do le wi li sp, absorb(code_model year market)

* 8. BLP instrument
sort market year
by market year: egen tot_sp = total(sp)
by market year: gen J = _N
ge sp_minus = tot_sp - sp
ge blp_sp = sp_minus/(J-1)

reghdfe price blp_sp cy we pl do le wi li sp, absorb(code_model year market)
ivreghdfe y (price=blp_sp) cy we pl do le wi li sp, absorb(code_model year market)
/*9. Now, you want to estimate the Costant Elasticity of Substition (CES) Demand
System for two different car models: Renault 4 (code_model == 196)

and quantity 1) the Rover Mini (code_model == 217). Obtain the parameter

keep if code_model == 196 | code_model == 13 | code_model == 217
