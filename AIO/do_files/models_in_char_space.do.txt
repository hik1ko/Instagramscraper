* here I need to compute the share of people that buy the product j
ge sj = sells/pop
* now I need to compute the share of people that do not buy anything
* the easiest way is to obtain it by doing 1 minus the share of people
* that buy one product
by year: egen tot_sells = total(sells)
ge sj_total = tot_sells/pop
ge s0 = 1- sj_total

* alternatively
by year: egen sj_tot = total(sj)
ge s0bis = 1 - sj_tot

* now I need to compute the dependent variable for models in characteristics space
ge y = log(sj) - log(s0)
* or equivalenty
ge ybis = log(sj/s0)

* regressions
regr y price

regr y price charact1 charact2 charact3


* BLP instrument
by year: egen sum_x = total(charact1)

ge x_tot_minus = sum_x - charact1
* how many products are in the market
by year: ge tot_prod = _N

ge blp = x_tot_minus/(tot_prod-1)

ivreg y (price = blp) charact1 charact2 charact3


* computing first stage regression 
reg price blp charact1 charact2 charact3
* test for weak instruments
test blp
